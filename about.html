About

JP group of educational institutions, a group comprising of various educational organizations in Western UP, Located at 5kms from the city centre and 65 kms from delhi is easily accessible by rail and road. It is about 2hours drive by road from the Delhi airport. As quality education can usher in an era of comprehensive development of all citizens of India, hence JP Educational Trust Society started its journey in the field of education by laying the foundation of a school, known as JP Academy; (Nursery to XII), a leading school in Uttar Pradesh. The JP Educational Trust Society also gave shape to professional colleges so that the youth can seek self satisfying jobs. Thus came up JP Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, JP Institute of Education, JP Institute of Applied Sciences and JP Institute of Engineering and Technology. The field of its vision was not restricted to Meerut and it saw a school JP International School in Greater Noida too. In tune with its thinking and vision is the creation of JP School of Business.

Our Inspiration

Late Smt. Krishna Agarwal w/o Sh. J. P. Agarwal the Chairman of the society, is the guiding soul and inspiration behind the creation of JP Academy. She dreamt of creating schools, where the student feels at home and education becomes a pleasurable and enjoyable experience. When she established her first school in Meerut, that is - JP Academy, she personally ensured that the students were provided the best available educational infrastructure in the country. Besides educational wherewithal she created salubrious environment with nature at its best manifestation. Smt. Krishna Agarwal believed that the beauty of nature coupled with parental like care by the teachers can create a true and inspiring educational ambience where the student willingly learns. We here, at JP Academy have committed our selves to turn her dreams into reality.

JP Agarwal

Chairman

The Academy as a co- educational Resident cum Day school came into existence in the year 1995 with Sh Atal Bihari Vajpayee our ex-Prime Minister laying its foundation stone. The school has since then marched on from success to success. It is a matter of great pride for me to see our alumni and alumnae occupying respectable positions in almost all walks of life and contributing to nation building. Our ex-students are not only doing well in the professional and occupational fields but have also excelled in Games and Sports too. The aim of education that we pursue at JP Academy is in consonance with high Indian ideals. According to Herbert Spencer, the ideal of ancient education in India was, "Training for completeness of life and moulding of character of men and women " And according to British Sanskrit scholar Arthur Anthony Macdonell (1854-1930) the aim of education in ancient India was the manifestation of divinity in Man. These aims touch the highest point of knowledge and we endeavour to follow these aims at J P Academy.

I understand that our Government and specially the CBSE Board is focusing on these ideals and lofty aims. I am sure with advent of CCE we will see young-men and women of rock like character being churned out of schools.

With profound belief and hope.

Dr. Hari Om Agarwal
