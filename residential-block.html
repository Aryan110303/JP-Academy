Residential Blocks

Residential Blocks

JP Academy being a day cum residential school has three boys hostels and one girls hostel which can accommodate more than 400 students. The hostels are beautifully constructed and are fitted with Geysers and Coolers. The school Gymnasium is located in the hostels and each hostel has self study room, TV room and separate warden rooms. Hostels are provided with ward boys and lady Ayahs for upkeep of dormitories and are supervised by Hostel Superintendents, ladies for girls hostel and gents for boys.

JP Education trust was established in the year 1993 under the dynamic leadership of Shri Jai Prakash Agarwal and Dr. Hariom Agarwal.
